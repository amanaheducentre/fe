# Daycare Program Images

Place the following images in this directory:

## Required Images:

1. **hero.jpg** - Hero background image
   - Recommended size: 1920x1080 or larger
   - Format: JPG or WebP
   - Subject: Daycare facility, children playing, or caregivers with children

2. **og-image.jpg** - Open Graph image for social media
   - Required size: 1200x630
   - Format: JPG
   - Subject: Same as hero or branded image

3. **gallery-1.jpg to gallery-6.jpg** - Gallery images
   - Recommended size: 800x800 or larger
   - Format: JPG or WebP
   - Subjects:
     - gallery-1: Main facility interior
     - gallery-2: Play area with toys
     - gallery-3: Sleeping area
     - gallery-4: Caregiver with children
     - gallery-5: Outdoor/activity area
     - gallery-6: Mealtime/feeding area

## Image Guidelines:

- Use high-quality, professional photos
- Ensure proper lighting and composition
- Show happy, engaged children (with parent consent)
- Highlight facility cleanliness and safety
- Include caregivers interacting with children
- Compress images before upload (use tools like TinyPNG)
